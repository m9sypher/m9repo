Points, Item Trades & Giveaway Policies

Applies to: Twitch · Kick · TikTok · YouTube  |  Last updated: June 2025  |  Version: 2.0
ℹ
These rules govern the M9Bot point economy, CS2 skin trade system, and stream giveaways. By participating in any of these features, you agree to all policies on this page. Moderators enforce these rules at their discretion.
Point System Overview § 1

M9Bot operates a stream point economy across all supported platforms. Points are the currency of the channel — they are earned by participating in chat, supporting the stream, and engaging with interactive features. Points have no real-world monetary value and cannot be sold or exchanged for real-world currency.

How Points Are Earned
Watching
Passive
Points accumulate while you're in chat during a live stream.
Chatting
Per Message
Active chatters earn bonus points for engaging with the stream.
Supporting
Bonus
Subs, Bits, Gifts, TikTok Diamonds — supporting grants extra point multipliers and exclusive perks.
Minigames
Win / Lose
Gambling minigames (roulette, slots, blackjack, case battles) can multiply or reduce your balance.

Point rates may fluctuate based on the live shop multiplier, which is displayed on-screen during the stream. The multiplier adjusts automatically based on shop activity and can rise during periods of low spending. The current multiplier is always visible in the stream overlay.

Spending Points
  • 1 Points can be spent in the M9Bot Point Shop to purchase CS2 skins, which are then delivered to your Steam inventory via automated trade offer.
  • 2 Points can be wagered in chat minigames including roulette, slots, blackjack, case openings, case battles, and the towers climbing game.
  • 3 Points can be used for channel point redemptions on Twitch where applicable.
  • 4 Points can also be used to submit item offers — proposing your own CS2 items to the bot in exchange for a points payout via the !offer system.
  • 5 Points do not expire while the stream is active, but M9Bot reserves the right to wipe or adjust balances in the event of abuse or system error.
✦
Supporter Perks: Subscribers, VIPs, channel supporters, and TikTok gifters receive bonus point rates, reduced cooldowns, and are exempt from certain message-threshold requirements across the bot's trade and giveaway features.
Item Trades for Points § 2

M9Bot's point shop allows viewers to spend accumulated points to receive real CS2 skins delivered directly to their Steam inventory via automated trade offer. The following rules govern all shop purchases and item deliveries. Read them carefully before making any purchase.

Setting Up Your Trade URL

To receive items purchased in the M9Bot shop, you must provide a valid Steam Trade URL. This URL links your Steam account to your chat identity so the bot can send trade offers directly to your inventory. Your Trade URL is required before any purchase can be processed.

A valid Steam Trade URL looks exactly like this:

Required format — Steam Trade URL https://steamcommunity.com/tradeoffer/new/?partner=XXXXXXXXX&token=XXXXXXXXX
  • 1 Your Trade URL must be obtained from your own Steam account under Profile → Trade Offers → Who can send me Trade Offers. Do not use a URL from any other source.
  • 2 The Trade URL must contain both a partner= value and a token= value. Incomplete or malformed URLs will be rejected and your purchase cannot proceed until corrected.
  • 3 You must ensure your Steam account is set to accept trade offers and that you are not trade-restricted on Steam's side (e.g. Steam Guard hold, Steam trade ban). M9Bot cannot bypass Steam-side restrictions.
  • 4 If your Trade URL changes (e.g. you regenerate your Steam token), you must re-submit your new URL before future purchases. Trades sent to an outdated URL may fail and cannot be reissued.
  • 5 Your Trade URL is linked to your chat identity and stored securely by M9Bot. Attempting to use a Trade URL that belongs to a different person's Steam account is a serious violation — see Trade Ban policy.
⚠
Never share your Steam login credentials to obtain a Trade URL. Your Trade URL is found in your Steam settings and does not require your password. M9Bot staff will never ask for your Steam password, Steam Guard code, or API key.
Trade URL Rules
  • 1 One Trade URL per account. Your Trade URL must consistently correspond to the same Steam account. Swapping your Trade URL to a different Steam account after it has been registered is not permitted.
  • 2 Do not paste someone else's Trade URL. Submitting a Trade URL that does not belong to your Steam account — regardless of how it was obtained — will result in an immediate trade ban. The trade will be voided and no refund will be issued.
  • 3 Expired or invalid tokens. If your Steam trade token has been regenerated and your saved URL is outdated, inform a moderator before attempting to re-submit. Simply re-submitting may be flagged for review.
All Point Purchases Are Final — No Refunds
🚫
All point purchases are final and non-refundable. Once points are deducted from your balance to initiate a shop purchase, those points cannot be returned under any circumstances.
  • 1 Trade reverts on Steam are not eligible for a refund. If you receive a trade offer and subsequently cancel or decline it — or if Steam automatically cancels the offer on your side — the points spent on that purchase will not be returned. Once M9Bot sends a trade offer, the transaction is considered complete from the point economy's perspective.
  • 2 Declined trade offers are not refunded. If you decline a trade offer for any reason (changed your mind, accepted on wrong account, etc.), no points will be restored.
  • 3 Expired trade offers are not refunded. Steam trade offers expire after a set period. If you fail to accept a trade offer before it expires, your points are forfeited. It is your responsibility to accept offers promptly.
  • 4 Steam-side holds or restrictions are not eligible for refunds. If your account has a Steam Guard trade hold, mobile authenticator hold, or any other Steam-imposed restriction that prevents item delivery, points cannot be refunded. Verify your Steam account is trade-eligible before making any purchase.
  • 5 Bot or technical errors that result in a failed trade will be reviewed on a case-by-case basis by a moderator. This is the only exception where a point restoration may be considered, and it is entirely at moderator discretion.

The no-refund policy exists to protect the integrity of the point economy and prevent abuse. Please ensure your Steam account is correctly configured and that you genuinely want an item before spending points. There are no exceptions to this policy outside of confirmed bot error.

Judgement System § 3

M9Bot runs a passive behaviour analysis system called Judgement that monitors chat activity across all platforms. It runs continuously in the background and assigns each user an internal verdict based on how they interact with the stream. You will not always be notified when you are evaluated.

The system is designed to distinguish genuine community members from accounts that show patterns consistent with exploitation, farming, or disruptive behaviour. Verdicts are not static — they update over time and are compared against your prior history. Positive engagement improves your standing; repeated problematic behaviour worsens it.

ℹ
We do not publicly disclose the specific behaviours that trigger Judgement. If you are acting in good faith and engaging genuinely with the stream, you have nothing to worry about. The system is not designed to penalise normal viewers.
Flags & Verdicts

If your behaviour trips the Judgement system, your account will be internally flagged. A flag does not immediately result in a ban or restriction — it places your account under heightened scrutiny. Moderators are notified and may review your activity manually.

Verdicts exist on a spectrum. A clean verdict has no impact on your ability to participate in the channel. A suspected verdict may restrict your eligibility for prizes or trade features pending moderator review. A confirmed bad verdict will result in restrictions being applied, up to and including a trade ban or channel ban depending on severity.

  • 1 First flag: Account is internally noted. No immediate action unless the behaviour is severe. You may continue using the channel normally.
  • 2 Repeated flags: Escalating verdicts are issued. Eligibility for giveaways, trades, and prize claims may be suspended pending moderator review.
  • 3 Confirmed bad verdict: Restrictions are applied. This may include a trade ban, removal from active giveaways, or a full channel ban depending on the nature of the behaviour.
⚠
Judgement flags can affect giveaway eligibility. If you are drawn as a giveaway winner but your account carries a bad or suspected verdict, a moderator may void your prize and trigger a re-roll. This is handled automatically and is not negotiable via chat.
Verdicts Can Improve

Judgement is not a one-strike system for most users. If your account was flagged due to early behaviour that has since changed, continued genuine participation over time will be reflected in updated verdicts. The system re-evaluates users periodically and compares current behaviour against past history. Accounts that demonstrate consistent, positive engagement can recover standing over time.

If you believe your account was incorrectly flagged, contact a moderator in chat. Moderators have the ability to manually review and override verdicts where appropriate.

Trade Bans § 4

M9Bot operates a trade ban system to protect the integrity of the point shop, item giveaways, and the channel community as a whole. A trade ban restricts a user from participating in item trades, shop purchases, and prize claims.

Trade bans may be applied automatically by M9Bot or manually by a moderator. A trade ban may be issued if we detect or suspect suspicious activity in connection with your account, your Trade URL, or your participation in trades or giveaways. Moderators reserve the right to issue a trade ban at any time, for any conduct they reasonably consider to be an attempt to exploit, abuse, or circumvent the system.

⚠
Suspicious activity is determined at moderator discretion. You do not need to have completed a prohibited action to receive a trade ban — patterns of behaviour, repeated anomalies, or any activity that undermines the fairness of the system may be sufficient grounds.

Examples of conduct that may result in a trade ban include, but are not limited to:

  • ✕ Submitting a Trade URL that does not belong to your own Steam account
  • ✕ Attempting to change your registered Trade URL to a different Steam account after it has been saved
  • ✕ Any activity M9Bot or a moderator identifies as an attempt to exploit, manipulate, or defraud the trade or point system
  • ✕ Coordinating with other users to abuse the trade or giveaway system
  • ✕ Repeated unusual patterns of behaviour in the shop or giveaway system that raise concern
  • ✕ Moderator-issued manual ban for conduct deemed harmful to the community or stream
Ban Tiers — Escalating Offences

Trade bans follow an escalating tier system. Each offence on your account increases the severity of the resulting ban. Offence history is permanently retained.

Offence # Duration Status Notes
1st Offence 10 Days Temporary Trade and shop access suspended for 10 days.
2nd Offence 30 Days Temporary Trade and shop access suspended for 30 days.
3rd Offence+ Permanent Permanent Lifetime ban from all M9Bot trade features. No appeal path.

While trade-banned, you cannot: purchase items from the point shop, enter or claim prizes in giveaways, or participate in any trade-based features. You can still chat, earn points, and play non-trade minigames depending on the specific ban circumstances.

ℹ
Message threshold: In addition to trade ban checks, participation in certain trade and giveaway features requires a minimum of 250 messages in the channel. Supporters, subscribers, VIPs, and channel supporters are exempt from this threshold.

If you believe a trade ban was issued in error, contact a moderator directly in chat. Moderators have the ability to review ban history and, where appropriate, remove an active ban. Disputes will not be handled via third-party platforms.

Giveaway Rules § 5

M9Bot runs live stream giveaways where a randomly selected winner receives a CS2 skin or other prize delivered via automated Steam trade. All giveaways are run on-stream and winners are drawn in real time. The following rules apply to all giveaways.

Entering a Giveaway
  • 1 To enter an active giveaway, type !giveaway in chat. Entry is available on all supported platforms (Twitch, Kick, TikTok, YouTube) simultaneously.
  • 2 One entry per person. Typing !giveaway more than once will not grant additional entries — duplicate entries are ignored automatically.
  • 3 You must not be trade banned to enter a giveaway. Trade-banned users are automatically excluded from entry and cannot claim prizes.
  • 4 Once a moderator starts the giveaway countdown with !startgiveaway, there is a 30-second final entry window. After the countdown ends, the entry list is locked and no new entries are accepted.
  • 5 The winner is selected using a random draw from all valid entries at the time of drawing. The selection is performed live on-stream.
Claiming Your Prize
  • 1 When your name is drawn, you will be announced in chat and given a time-limited window (typically 2 minutes) to paste your Steam Trade URL in chat.
  • 2 The Trade URL you submit must belong to your own Steam account. Submitting a Trade URL from any other Steam account is a violation of the trade rules and will result in your prize being voided and a trade ban applied.
  • 3 If you have a Trade URL already on file with M9Bot, it must match the URL you submit. Attempting to switch to a different Steam account to claim a prize will void the giveaway and result in a trade ban.
  • 4 If you do not paste your Trade URL within the claim window, the trade window expires and the prize is forfeited. A re-roll is required and will be performed at moderator discretion via !startgiveaway.
  • 5 You must have at least 250 messages in the channel to be eligible to claim a prize. If you win but do not meet this threshold, your prize will be voided and a re-roll will be performed. Supporters, subscribers, VIPs, and TikTok gifters are exempt from this requirement.
  • 6 Once your Trade URL is accepted and the trade is launched, the prize is considered delivered. Trade reverts, declined offers, and expired Steam trade offers do not entitle you to a re-draw or re-issue of the prize.
  • 7 Accounts flagged by the moderation system for suspicious activity may be deemed ineligible to claim prizes at moderator discretion, triggering a re-roll.
ℹ
Re-rolls: If a winner is ineligible (trade banned, below message threshold, expired window, suspicious flag), a moderator will perform a re-roll by restarting the draw. Re-rolls pull from the same original entry list.
General Giveaway Notes
  • ● Giveaway prizes and frequency are determined entirely by the streamer. There is no guaranteed schedule.
  • ● Moderators may void an entire giveaway at any time using !voidgiveaway, clearing all entries and cancelling any pending trade window.
  • ● Attempting to manipulate a giveaway through any means — including multi-account entry, coercion, or exploit — will result in an immediate trade ban and removal from the current and future giveaways.
  • ● M9Bot giveaways are a community feature, not a promotional lottery. No purchase is necessary to enter and no guarantee of winning is implied.
🚫
All giveaway prizes are final once delivered. Prizes cannot be exchanged, transferred, or returned for points after a trade has been sent. The same no-refund policy that applies to point shop purchases applies to all giveaway prizes.
