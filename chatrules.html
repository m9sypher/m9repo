Country / Nationality Hatred
No hatred, mockery, or discrimination targeting someone's country or nationality. CS is a global game — this chat is global. You can talk trash about a match, not about where someone is from. Violations follow the standard ladder: warn → mute → mute → perma-ban.

Racism, hate speech, and slurs targeting race, ethnicity, religion, or identity are an instant ban. No ladder, no warnings — you're gone. M9_BOT will act if it catches it; mods will act if it doesn't.

No threats or sustained harassment toward any viewer, mod, or the streamer. Joking with someone is fine; going after them is not. We'll know the difference.
