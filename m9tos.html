System Overview

M9 Systems are a collection of custom-built automation, loyalty, and entertainment systems used on this channel.

  • All systems are provided for entertainment and community engagement
  • No system represents real money, banking, or stored value
  • Participation is voluntary
  • Systems may change, pause, or be removed at any time

Bots, Automation & AI

Bot Identity

  • This channel uses a custom automated bot named M9_BOT
  • M9_BOT is a system-controlled account and is not a human user

Bot Capabilities

  • M9_BOT may post messages, respond to chat, and process commands
  • M9_BOT may run games, track points, and manage reward systems
  • M9_BOT may initiate messages during idle periods

AI Behavior

  • M9_BOT may use AI-assisted logic to generate responses
  • AI behavior is restricted, moderated, and safety-guarded
  • M9_BOT does not impersonate users, moderators, or staff
  • M9_BOT may refuse, deflect, or disengage from certain topics
Messages sent by M9_BOT represent automated system behavior and not human intent or advice.

Chat Relaying & Cross-Platform Messages

Relayed Messages

  • Messages from external platforms may be relayed into chat by M9_BOT
  • All relayed messages are posted by M9_BOT, not by real Twitch users
  • Relayed messages are clearly formatted as quoted or external content

Visual Overlays

  • External usernames may appear visually in overlays
  • Overlay presentation is visual-only
  • Twitch chat remains the authoritative source of authorship
M9_BOT is the only account posting relayed messages into Twitch chat.

Point System

Nature of Points

  • Points are fictional and server-side
  • Points are not real money or currency
  • Points are non-transferable
  • Points cannot be purchased

Point Usage

  • Points may be spent in games
  • Points may be lost through gameplay
  • Points are deducted for reward redemptions and sound spams, but not upon a giveaway win
Points themselves have no monetary value, but may be used to request digital rewards at the channel’s discretion.

Earning Points

  • Chat activity and engagement
  • Daily reward commands
  • True first-time follows only
  • Channel subscriptions (perk-based)
  • Special events or promotions

Safeguards

  • Re-follows do not grant points
  • Existing balances are not affected by re-follows
  • Suspicious or abusive behavior may be logged
Subscriptions provide Twitch-native benefits first. Points are a bonus perk, not a purchase.

Games & Gambling

Gameplay

  • Participation is optional
  • Games use fictional points only
  • Some games may involve chance-based outcomes

Loss & Risk

  • Users may lose points
  • Losing points does not mean losing money
  • No deposits or purchases are required
Points can always be re-earned through normal participation.

Rewards & Real-World Digital Value

Reward Types

  • Digital game skins or cosmetics
  • Digital gift cards
  • Digital game copies or licenses

Redemption Process

  • Redemption is user-initiated
  • Points are deducted upon approved redemption
  • Rewards are not guaranteed
  • Fulfillment may be manual
  • Trade URLs or account details may be required
Rewards are prizes or giveaways — not cash-outs, withdrawals, or winnings.

What This System Is Not

  • No buying points
  • No Bits-to-points conversion
  • No deposits
  • No withdrawals
  • No guaranteed financial outcomes
