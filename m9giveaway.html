Important Notice

All giveaways are for entertainment purposes only and reward genuine, active community members. Participation does not constitute a financial transaction or guarantee of any prize.

Any attempt to manipulate watch time, engagement, or entry systems will result in permanent disqualification.

01 — Purpose
🎯
Purpose & Intent

M9 Systems conducts giveaways solely to reward active, engaged community members across Twitch, Kick, and TikTok — for entertainment and community engagement, nothing more.

By participating, you agree that eligibility is determined by watch time, engagement, and compliance with these rules. M9 Systems maintains full authority to deny, revoke, or void any entry at our discretion — including after a winner has been selected.

02 — Eligibility
⏱️
Watch Time
1 hr min

To claim a prize you must have at least 1 hour of verified watch time. Watch time accumulates when:

  • Stream is live and online
  • Stream volume is at or above 1%
  • You send messages in chat within a 5-minute activity window
Check your watch time anytime using !check YourName in chat.
💙
Twitch Follow
Required

Twitch users must be following the channel to enter. Follow status is verified automatically when you type !giveaway. Non-followers are notified and denied entry.

🚫
Prohibited Accounts
  • Bot accounts, multi-accounts, and automated scripts — strictly prohibited
  • Coordinating entries across accounts is detectable and a permanent ban offence
  • Moderators may participate at the streamer's discretion but are not exempt from watch time
03 — Ticket System
🎟️
Entry Ticket
!giveaway

Type !giveaway in chat to enter. You receive 1 ticket automatically. This is your base entry — additional tickets improve your odds but never guarantee a win.

💬
Activity Tickets
Auto-earned

Every 10 minutes of genuine chat activity earns you an additional ticket automatically — no command needed. There is no maximum on activity-earned tickets.

Idle presence without messaging does not qualify.

🪙
Buy Tickets
🪙12.49 each

Use !buytickets <amount> to purchase tickets at 🪙12.49 each. Maximum 100 purchased tickets per stream session — resets each new stream.

Note: Purchased tickets improve odds but do not bypass watch time or integrity checks.
📊
Check Tickets
!chance

Type !chance to see your current count. The live overlay on stream also shows total entrants, total tickets in the pool, and top ticket holders in real time.

Tickets are non-transferable, have no monetary value, and persist across multiple draws within a stream session.

Tickets reset when the server restarts between streams.
04 — Entry
📝
How to Enter
!giveaway
  • Type !giveaway in chat on Twitch, Kick, or TikTok
  • One base entry per user — additional chances through the ticket system only
  • Requirements are verified at the time of winning, not at entry
  • Twitch users must be following at the time of entry
05 — Winner Selection
🏆
Draw Process

Winners are selected from the weighted ticket pool. More tickets = more slots = better odds. The draw samples the pool multiple times and picks randomly from those results — no outcome is ever guaranteed regardless of ticket count.

Watch time and engagement are verified automatically at selection. Failing entries are disqualified and a new winner is selected.

🔒
Integrity System
Always On

A multi-layered behavioural analysis system monitors participation continuously across all platforms — analysing engagement quality, message authenticity, session history, and cross-account relationships.

Entries flagged by this system may be voided without notice or appeal. Thresholds and signals are intentionally undisclosed. Genuine participants have nothing to worry about.

06 — Claiming
⏱️
60 Second Window
  • Upon being announced, you have 60 seconds to paste your Steam Trade URL in chat
  • The URL must match the account registered under your username
  • A trade offer is sent automatically once verified
  • Missing the window forfeits the prize — a re-roll is triggered
  • Your tickets are preserved after a missed claim
Register your Steam Trade URL by pasting it in chat during any shop purchase or claim window.
07 — Prohibited
🚨
Prohibited Behaviour
  • Multiple accounts to increase entry chances — prohibited and detectable
  • Coordinating entry attempts across accounts
  • Participating only during giveaway periods with no genuine engagement
  • Using bots, scripts, or automated tools — permanent ban
  • Attempting to transfer, sell, or trade a giveaway win
  • Tickets improve odds — they do not override verification requirements
