M9 Systems – Giveaway Terms & Participation Rules

Purpose & Intent

M9 Systems conducts giveaways solely to reward **active, engaged community members**. These giveaways are strictly for **entertainment, channel support, and engagement purposes**. Any attempt to bypass or manipulate the system will result in **immediate disqualification**.

By participating, you explicitly agree that eligibility is determined by **watch time, engagement, and compliance** with the rules outlined herein. We maintain the authority to **deny, revoke, or void any entries** at our discretion.

Eligibility & Watch Time Tracking

  • Only users **actively participating in the channel** are eligible to enter giveaways.
  • To claim a prize, a user must have **at least 30 minutes of verified watch time**.
  • Watch time is **tracked and stored securely on our server** and accumulates under the following conditions:
    • When the stream is **online/up**.
    • When the stream volume is **≥1%**.
    • When the user sends messages in chat, even if the stream volume is muted, **within a 5-minute window** per message batch.
  • All three triggers above are **independent but cumulative**. For example:
    • If the stream is up and volume is ≥1%, watch time updates automatically.
    • If the stream is up but volume is 0% and the user is actively typing messages, watch time still updates based on chat activity.
    • If the stream is up, volume is ≥1%, and the user is also sending messages, watch time updates as a combination of all valid triggers.
  • Bot accounts, multi-accounts, or automated scripts are strictly prohibited.
  • Channel moderators or staff may participate **at the discretion of the streamer**, but they are not exempt from watch time rules.
Watch time tracking ensures that giveaways reward **real, engaged viewership**, not passive or artificially inflated activity.

Entry & Participation

  • Entries must be submitted using the official chat command, e.g., !giveaway.
  • Only one entry per user is allowed per giveaway unless explicitly stated otherwise.
  • Users must remain compliant with chat rules and channel guidelines during the giveaway period.
  • Entry does not guarantee eligibility if watch time or activity requirements are not met.

Winner Selection & Verification

  • Winners are selected **randomly** from the pool of eligible participants.
  • The system automatically verifies watch time and engagement via !check or internal server metrics.
  • Entries failing to meet minimum requirements will be **disqualified**, and a new winner will be chosen.
  • Winners are notified through chat or platform DMs depending on the type of giveaway.
  • All decisions regarding eligibility, selection, and verification are **final and non-negotiable**.
Users are encouraged to verify their watch time regularly using !check YourNameHere to ensure compliance.

Claiming Your Prize & CODEWORD System

  • To fully claim a prize, a **CODEWORD visual** will appear **periodically on stream during the giveaway**. Users must **actively view the stream** to capture it.
  • The correct redemption command is:
    CODEWORD <YourSteamTradeURL> if you have met the watch time and engagement requirements.
  • The CODEWORD is a type of **image/visual** — users must submit its **name exactly**, but **letter casing does not matter**. For example, "CaRROT", "carrOT", and "carrot" are all valid.
  • Mentioning the CODEWORD directly in chat outside of the redemption command may result in **disqualification**. This prevents bot accounts or automated scripts from harvesting or filtering CODEWORD entries from chat messages.
  • Users must submit the CODEWORD correctly to ensure prize redemption; partial or incorrect submissions will be rejected.
The CODEWORD system is designed to **verify real-time engagement** with the stream. Treat it as a mandatory, real-time participation checkpoint.
Important: All giveaways are for **entertainment purposes only**. Participation does not constitute a financial transaction, investment, or guarantee of any prize. Any attempt to manipulate watch time, engagement, or the CODEWORD system will result in **permanent disqualification**.
