Giveaway Terms &
Participation Rules

By entering any M9 Systems giveaway, you agree to the terms outlined below. All decisions are final.

⚠

Important: All giveaways are for entertainment purposes only and serve to reward genuine, active community members. Participation does not constitute a financial transaction, investment, or guarantee of any prize. Any attempt to manipulate watch time, engagement, or entry systems will result in permanent disqualification.

01 Purpose & Intent

M9 Systems conducts giveaways solely to reward active, engaged community members across Twitch, Kick, and TikTok. These giveaways exist for entertainment, channel support, and community engagement — nothing more.

By participating, you explicitly agree that eligibility is determined by watch time, engagement, and compliance with the rules outlined herein. M9 Systems maintains full authority to deny, revoke, or void any entry at our discretion, including after a winner has been selected.

02 Eligibility & Watch Time
  • Only users actively participating in the channel are eligible to enter giveaways.
  • To claim a prize, a user must have at least 30 minutes of verified watch time on record.
  • Watch time accumulates under the following independent, cumulative conditions:
    • Stream is live and online
    • Stream volume is at or above 1%
    • User sends messages in chat within a 5-minute activity window, even if stream is muted
  • Bot accounts, multi-accounts, and automated scripts are strictly prohibited and will result in permanent disqualification.
  • Channel moderators may participate at the streamer's discretion but are not exempt from watch time requirements.
Verify your watch time anytime using !check YourName in chat. Watch time tracking ensures giveaways reward real, engaged viewership — not passive or artificially inflated activity.
03 Entry & Participation
  • Entries must be submitted using the official chat command !giveaway during an active giveaway period.
  • Only one entry per user is permitted per giveaway unless explicitly stated otherwise.
  • Users must remain compliant with chat rules and channel guidelines throughout the giveaway period.
  • Entering does not guarantee eligibility — watch time and engagement requirements are verified at the time of winning.
  • Entries are accepted across Twitch Kick TikTok platforms simultaneously.
04 Winner Selection & Verification
  • Winners are selected randomly from the pool of eligible participants.
  • Watch time and engagement are automatically verified at the point of selection via internal server metrics.
  • Entries failing to meet minimum requirements will be disqualified and a new winner selected.
  • Winners are notified through chat — they must respond within 60 seconds with a valid Steam Trade URL to claim their prize.
  • Trade URLs are verified against our records — a URL registered to a different user will result in immediate void and re-roll.
  • All decisions regarding eligibility, selection, and verification are final and non-negotiable.
Automated Integrity System

M9 Systems operates an internal behaviour analysis system that monitors participation patterns across all platforms. This system runs silently in the background and is not visible to participants. At the point of winning, an internal review is automatically conducted — entries flagged by this system may be voided without prior notice. Participants have no ability to view, appeal, or access the results of this review. The system exists solely to protect the integrity of the giveaway for genuine community members.

The integrity system is fully automated and operates across session history, engagement patterns, and platform behaviour. Genuine community members have nothing to worry about.
05 Claiming Your Prize
  • Upon being announced as winner in chat, you have 60 seconds to respond with your Steam Trade URL.
  • Your Steam Trade URL must be submitted directly in chat — it is automatically detected by our system.
  • The Trade URL must match the account registered under your username. Mismatched URLs will void the claim.
  • Once a valid URL is received, a trade offer will be sent to your Steam account automatically.
  • Failure to respond within the 60-second window results in prize forfeiture and a mandatory re-roll.
To register or update your Steam Trade URL, simply paste it in chat during a shop purchase or giveaway claim window. Your URL is securely stored on our server and linked to your account.
06 Prohibited Behaviour
  • Creating or operating multiple accounts to increase giveaway entry chances is strictly prohibited.
  • Coordinating entry attempts with other users across accounts is prohibited and detectable.
  • Participating only during giveaway periods with no genuine channel engagement is grounds for disqualification.
  • Using bots, scripts, or automated tools of any kind is a permanent ban offence.
  • Attempting to transfer, sell, or trade a giveaway win to another user will result in immediate void.
