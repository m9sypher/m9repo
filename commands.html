📱 TikTok users: Commands do not require the ! prefix — just type the command name directly, e.g. points instead of !points
💰 Points & Economy 6 commands ▼
!points [user]Check your or another user's point balance
!give <user> <amount>Give points to another user — use their platform ID (e.g. twitch_username)
!topShow the top 5 point balances across all platforms
!dailyClaim your daily point drop — resets every 24 hours
!rainCheck the current rain pot — distributed to active chatters every 30 min
!tiprain <amount>Tip points into the rain pot for everyone to share
⭐ Leveling 1 command ▼
!level [user]Check your or another user's level, XP, and progress to the next level — cap is 1000
⏱️ Watchtime 2 commands ▼
!watchtime [user]Check how long you or another user has watched the stream
!check [user]Shorthand for !watchtime
🎮 Games 17 commands ▼
!war <amount>Draw a card vs the dealer — highest wins. Min 1 / Max 250. Higher wager = harder dealer
!slots <wager>Spin 3 reels — match symbols to win
!scratch <wager>Scratch 9 tiles — match 5+ symbols to win. Full page = ×500. Min 1 / Max 250
!roulette <red/black/green> <amount>Bet on red, black, or green. Red/Black = 2×, Green = 10×. Min 1 / Max 250
!coinflip <wager>Flip a coin — win 2× or lose it all. Min 1 / Max 250
!duel <amount>Open a duel — winner takes both wagers
!join <code>Join an open duel by its code
!duelsList all currently open duels
!towers <wager>Start a tower climb — 15 floors, 4 doors per floor. Min 1 / Max 1000
!climbAttempt the next floor of your active tower run
!bailCash out your current tower multiplier and leave safely
!verifytowersReview your last tower run — floor-by-floor breakdown
!toptowersShow the top 5 tower winners this week
!vaultCheck the current vault pot and usage — costs 1.25 pts per attempt
!vault <4-digit combo>Guess the vault combination — correct guess wins the entire pot
!blackjack <wager>Play blackjack against the dealer — !hit !stand !double available mid-hand
!gamesList all available games with usage info

!sell <item> <qty>Sell items back to the market e.g. !sell rock 2 — selling lowers that item's price
