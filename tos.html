Stream Engagement System

M9sypheRPoints Terms

How the stream loyalty point system works, what it is, and what it isn't.

Effective as of 2026 · m9sypher.github.io

01 — What Points Are

Stream Engagement Currency

Points are a free, non-purchasable loyalty currency earned exclusively through participation in the stream. They have no monetary value, cannot be bought with real money on any platform, and exist solely to reward community engagement.

Points cannot be transferred to real money under any circumstance. There is no cash-out mechanism, no withdrawal system, and no conversion to any currency inside this system.

02 — How Points Are Earned

Free Engagement Only

Every way to earn points in this system is free and engagement-based. No purchase is ever required or accepted for points.

Chat Activity Points accumulate passively while actively chatting in the stream.
Rain Pot A shared pot grows with every chat message and distributes automatically to active viewers every 30 minutes.
Leveling XP earned through chat rewards level-up bonuses at milestone levels.
Games Wagering points in stream games (Towers, Vault, Slots etc.) can increase or decrease your balance.
Item Offers Users may submit a voluntary item offer for streamer review. See Section 05 for full details.
03 — The Point Shop

Gifts, Not Prizes

The Point Shop allows viewers to redeem points for CS2 skins and Steam games owned by the streamer. These redemptions are gifts from the streamer, not prizes won through gambling.

All shop transactions are entirely at the streamer's discretion. Items can go out of stock, trades can expire, and the streamer reserves the right to void any transaction at any time for any reason.

Redemption of points for items does not constitute a guaranteed exchange, contract, or financial instrument of any kind.

What happens to an item after delivery is outside the scope of this system. Any secondary sale or transfer of received items occurs on third-party platforms (Steam Marketplace) and is the sole responsibility of the viewer.

04 — Stream Games

Entertainment Only

Stream games (Towers, Vault, Slots, Blackjack, etc.) allow viewers to wager points for entertainment. Because points have no real monetary value and cannot be purchased, these games do not constitute gambling under any standard legal definition.

05 — Item Offers (!offer)

Voluntary Item Submissions

Viewers may optionally submit a CS2 item they own to the streamer in exchange for stream points using the !offer command. This feature operates under the following conditions:

Voluntary Submissions are entirely optional. No viewer is required or pressured to submit an item.
User-Defined Price The viewer sets their own asking price in points. The streamer does not assign value to items.
Manual Approval Every offer requires manual review and explicit approval by the streamer. Nothing is automated.
Streamer Discretion The streamer may decline any offer at any time without reason. Declined offers carry no obligation.
30 Minute Window Offers expire after 30 minutes if not reviewed. Expired offers are automatically cancelled.
Not a Deposit System This is not an automated item-to-credit deposit system. It is a voluntary, human-reviewed exchange at the viewer's discretion.

Item offers do not create a closed loop. Points received through an accepted offer are the same stream engagement points as those earned through chatting — they carry no guaranteed redemption value and cannot be converted to cash.

06 — What This System Is Not

Clear Boundaries

Not a Casino No real money enters this system at any point. Points cannot be purchased.
Not a Gambling Site Stream games use non-monetary engagement points. No consideration of real value is wagered.
Not a Skin Gambling Site Items received from the shop are gifts, not gambling winnings. There is no automated deposit-to-wager pipeline.
Not a Financial Service Points are not an investment, financial instrument, or store of value of any kind.
Not a Marketplace The Point Shop is a streamer gift program, not a commercial marketplace or exchange.
07 — Platform Compliance

Built Within the Rules

This system is designed to comply with the terms of service of all platforms it operates on, including Twitch, Kick, TikTok, and YouTube. Stream engagement reward systems are explicitly supported by these platforms.

Steam trading is conducted manually — the streamer physically confirms every trade offer. No automated trading bots or scripts bypass Steam's trade confirmation requirements.

08 — Changes & Contact

Updates

These terms may be updated at any time. Continued participation in the stream's point system after any update constitutes acceptance of the revised terms.

Questions about these terms can be directed to the streamer via stream chat or the community Discord.

By participating in the stream's point system, viewers acknowledge that points have no monetary value, are not exchangeable for cash, and that all shop items received are gifts at the streamer's sole discretion.
